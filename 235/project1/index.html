About

Hello, my name is Kevin Salvador Vasquez. I am a programmer specializing in video game development, proficient in C#, C/C++, JavaScript, Java, and Lua. I am a Game Design and Delevopment Major at RIT, currently seeking a co-op opportunity.

Creation is my passion. Programming, writing, illustrating, animating, and designing are the tools by which I sculpt my ideas into works for others to enjoy, like clay in my hands. It is my wish to craft entire worlds and share them in hopes to inspire others to do same. This is my object of obsession.

In my free time, I also enjoy studying languages, both ancient and modern, as well as baking pastries.
